About Us

We are a new age corporate brand. The business fraternity recognizes us as a core technology company with a strong DNA towards solution creation and innovation. Creating solutions for end users to enhance their business processes is our sole priority. It has helped us to explore technology to its fullest business application. The capability to innovate continuously has kept us ahead of our competitors. It has supported us to adapt to ever-changing business parameters. The deep-rooted and limitless technology heritage connects all our businesses through a common chord. For almost twenty five years our brand has helped all our stakeholders to foresee, steer and benefit from the change taking place in the industry. During this period, we have been working with the main objective of being the frontier of specialized technologies which provide high value services to our customers. This has made us the top technology provider to global software product companies across a wide spectrum of industries. Our product engineering services have become inevitable for the global players. These services suit the present day business specifications. Because of these innovative services we have clients from US, Canada and Europe. With over 5000 person-years of experience and more than US$ 25 million of investment in developing software products, we promise to bring futuristic technologies that further push the boundary of technology innovation.
